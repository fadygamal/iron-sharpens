A Journey Toward the Promise

Iron Sharpens Iron

Every journey toward what God has called you to runs straight through difficult people — accusers, manipulators, betrayers, doubters, rivals. This game walks you through 13 real-feeling encounters, each one mirrored by a figure from Scripture who faced the same thing.

There's no single right answer — but some responses build real character (Patience, Wisdom, Forgiveness, Courage) and some quietly drain it. Watch the jars.
